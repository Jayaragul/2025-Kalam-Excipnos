Round 1: PRESENTATION

This round focuses on your presentation skills. You will need to present your ideas clearly, explain your project's vision, and provide a live demonstration of its functionality.

Round 2: PROTOTYPE

In this round, you will need to build a functional prototype of your project. The prototype must be a working model that demonstrates your project's core functionality and feasibility.

Round 3: VALIDATION AND WORKING MODEL

This is the final round where the participants need to show their working model of their project and the judges evaluate based on the problem solved for the real world scenario.
