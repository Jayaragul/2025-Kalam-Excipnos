Excipnos - AI & DS Association

Excipnos, the AI & DS Association of SIET, is committed to fostering innovation, learning, and collaboration. As part of Kalam, we organize a diverse range of events, including hackathons, technical competitions, non-technical events, and workshops, designed to inspire creativity and enhance problem-solving skills. Our goal is to provide a platform for students to explore AI & Data Science, engage with industry experts, and gain hands-on experience through practical challenges. Whether you're a tech enthusiast or looking to explore new domains, Excipnos offers opportunities for all to learn, compete, and excel.
